Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

This is an actual recipe that I use regularly lol, heres the things you'll need!

  • 1 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1 cup of bread flour
  • 3/4 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp of fine salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup of dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp of expresso powder
  • 1 egg and 1 egg yolk
  • Chocolate chips

Skipping the long story about my life or whatever (why do baking websites do that lol?)

Start by browning your butter on the stove in a saucepan. You want to do this on medium/low heat. You'll know its browned when it gets a nice golden/dark color and starts to smell kinda caramel-ly and makes your house smell really delicious.
Once that's done set it aside to chill and kinda solidify a bit.

In another bowl, sift together your bread flour, all purpose flour, salt, and baking soda and set it aside. Combine your sugars, vanilla, expresso powder, and brown butter together in a seperate bowl and mix. Add the egg and egg yolk and mix. Gradually add your dry ingredients until a dough forms, then add your chocolate chips.

This step is optional but I would recommend you chill your dough for at least 30 minutes to set and rest for a bit but you can bake right away if you prefer. Bake at 350F for 12-14 minutes or until golden brown.
