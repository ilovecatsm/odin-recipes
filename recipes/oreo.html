Reeses Cookies Recipe

This is yet another recipe that I use regularly (I love baking), heres the things you'll need!

  • 1 cup of room temp unsalted butter
  • Crushed oreos
  • 2 3/4 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp of fine salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 cups of brown sugar
  • 3/4 cups of white sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • Chocolate chips

Start by creaming together your butter and sugars. Mix your eggs and vanilla into the mixture.

In a bowl, combine your all purpose flour, salt, and baking soda. Add your dry ingredients and mix until a dough forms, then add your chocolate chips and crushed oreos.

Bake at 350F for 12-13 minutes.
